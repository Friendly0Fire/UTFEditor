{\rtf1{\b\ul KEYS}\par
\par
1            Bottom view\par
2            Top view\par
3            Back view\par
4            Front view\par
5            Right view\par
6            Left view\par
\par
B            Toggle background between white & black\par
P            Toggle the panel\par
\par
*            Increase size of hardpoint display\par
/            Decrease size of hardpoint display\par
+            Zoom in\par
-            Zoom out\par
\par
Arrows       Move the model (with Shift for finer movement)\par
Page Up      Rotate 15° (1° with Shift) anticlockwise around the Y axis\par
Page Down    Rotate 15° (1° with Shift) clockwise around the Y axis\par
Ctrl+Page    Rotate around the X axis\par
Home         Move to center, remove rotation\par
\par
{\b\ul EDIT KEYS}\par
Space		Enter Edit Mode\par
A, D		Rotate selected hardpoint 15° (1° with Shift) around the Y axis\par
W, X		Rotate around the X axis\par
Q, E		Rotate around the Z axis\par
Z, C		Increase (decrease with Ctrl) min or max by 5° (1° with Shift) for selected revolute hardpoint\par
\par
{\b\ul ADD KEYS}\par
Ctrl+Space	Enter Add Mode\par
Enter		Move to next hardpoint\par
N		Switch to previous hardpoint type\par
M		Switch to next hardpoint type\par
\par
{\b\ul MOUSE}\par
\par
Left         Rotate around the Y axis (left/right) and X axis (up/down)\par
Right        Zoom out/in (up/down) or rotate about the Z axis (left/right)\par
Both         Move the model (or Shift+Left)\par
Wheel        Zoom out/in (up/down)\par
\par
}
